About me

Abhishek Vasudev, a professional motorcycle racer, representing Honda Racing India, has been racing for the past 9 years and has 4 championship tittles, latest being the one in 2019 in 201-300 cc Indian National Road Racing Championship. Also known as 'MadRab33', Abhishek is famously associated as the Mad Rabbit of the Indian circuit racing scene. Highly commemorated for putting up a good fight, MadRab33 is everyone's favorite maverick to watch in action.
